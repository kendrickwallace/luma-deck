Show Creator

Build a light show in six steps.

A guided wizard walks you from source to save. Assign a scene at any timestamp, drop in a special effect, and you're live.

STEP 1

Choose a Source

Sync to a video, an audio track, or a countdown timer from 15 minutes to 6 hours.

STEP 2

Check Your Lights

Review connection status across every bridge and Wi-Fi light, then refresh in a tap.

STEP 3

Pick a Group

Select which rooms or light groups the show will control — one or many at once.

STEP 4

Build the Timeline

Scrub to any moment and drop in a scene or a special effect like Thunderstorm or Volcano.

STEP 5

Add Triggers

Place manual trigger buttons to fire dramatic effects on demand during playback.

STEP 6

Name & Save

Review, name your show, and save. It lands in the Control tab, ready to play.
